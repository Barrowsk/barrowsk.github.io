Early-Stage AI Company*

*name not included for privacy purposes

#Development

Early-Stage AI Company is a startup whose main product is a mobile app that allows users to upload videos of workers and use AI to automate ergonomic assessments. Working with another developer from Cardinal Labs, we built out the Risk Suite, a web app counterpart to the mobile app. This included creating video upload, form submit, video display features, and a dashboard for administrators to manage employee groups and videos. The most challenging task was to work out how to upload and securely stream user videos as well as display the video’s data analysis on a synchronized graph. More info about this on the Cardinal Labs website.

Tools: React, Redux, Firebase

Stanford Student Enterprises

#Design #Development

sse.stanford.edu

My first project with Cardinal Labs was implementing the Stanford Student Enterprises (SSE) website redesign. SSE manages five student-run businesses which fund Stanford's student life. As one of SSE’s enterprises, Cardinal Labs conducted a ground-up remodel of the website with emphasis on communicating the organization’s values to the larger Stanford community. Working with a development team, we used Webflow to bring our mockups (created on Figma) to life. Webflow also allows SSE to easily make changes and edit the site whenever they need without much technical knowledge. More info about this on the Cardinal Labs website.

Tools: Figma, Webflow
